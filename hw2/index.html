Shameless

Shameless is an American comedy drama television series developed by John Wells that aired on Showtime
from January 9, 2011, to April 11, 2021. It is an adaptation of Paul Abbott's British series of the same name and
features an ensemble cast led by William H. Macy and Emmy Rossum. The show is set on the South Side of Chicago, Illinois.

Plot

The series tells about the dysfunctional family of Frank Gallagher, a single father, alcoholic and drug addict, who has six
children - Fiona, Philip, Ian, Debbie, Carl and Liam. While he spends his days drunk, high, or seeking misadventure, his children
are learning to live on their own. A subplot focuses on their neighbors and the Chicago area they live in.

Main characters

  • Francis "Frank" Gallagheris an alcoholic, selfish, liar, petty swindler, and a confirmed parasite.
  • Fiona Gallagher is Frank's middle daughter.
  • James "Jimmy" Lishman / Steve Wilton / Jack is Fiona's boyfriend.
  • Philip "Lip" Ronan Gallagher is the eldest of the brothers.
  • Ian Clayton Gallagher is the middle brother.
  • William "Liam" Gallagher is the youngest in the family.
  • Deborah "Debbie" Gallagher is the youngest of the daughters.
  • Carl Francis Gallagher is one of the brothers.
  • Veronica "Vee" Fisher is a close friend of Fiona.
  • Kevin "Kev" Ball is Veronica's husband.
  • Sheila Jackson is an agoraphobic housewife who enjoys cooking.
  • Karen Jackson is Lip's frivolous and promiscuous girlfriend.
  • Svetlana Evgenievna Milkovich Fisher is a bisexual Russian prostitute living illegally in America.
  • Samantha "Sammy" Slott is Frank's eldest daughter by one of his girlfriends from his youth.
